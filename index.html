THE ARTIST REVIEWS

We love music

MuseTech is an online site that reviews new music to keep you in the know about the latest artists. At the end of each month, we update the Reviews on our site with our favourite finds. Our reviews give you insight on who each artist is, their latest album, and our favourite song. See our ABOUT US to learn more about the people behind MuseTech. Stay tuned for more reviews!

Billy Raffoul

Album: Born to Die

Billy Raffoul is a Canadian rock singer-songwriter. He grew up in a family of musicians, which introduced him to the music business...Read More

First releasing music in 2017, the singer has experienced rising success. He has collaborated with boundary breaking artists such as Julia Michaels, Avicii, and Kygo and opened for Kings of Leon, X Ambassadors, and NEEDTOBREATHE.

This artist with a unique sound has recently picked up some traction with the release of his new EP Borne to Die. He was also the winner of the 2021 SOCAN Songwriting prize for his single “Western Skies.” We are excited to see what else Billy Raffoul has to offer!

EP: Borne to Die
The mix of JJ Wilde and Billy Raffoul’s voices create a beautiful raspy melody that makes the newly released Borne to Die EP an addicting listen. The EP features 3 songs with staggering variety, allowing for an engaging and interesting listen.

Our Favourite Song
Musetech’s favourite song from the album is the anthemic rock song that matches the title of the EP, “Born to Die.” The song features sentimental lyrics that tell a love story and pulls at the heartstrings. With a catchy tune and conversational tone, this song is also a fan-favourite on the EP.

Chris Larkin

Album: Greenpath

Christopher Larkin is a composer and sound designer for film, television, and video games. The artist has always had a love of creativity...Read More and began exploring the medium of music making in his early teens. Now, a full time composer, he is best known for his score and sound for indie game hit “Hollow Knight”, sound for “Pac-Man 256”, and recently the sweeping orchestral score for feature documentary “Barbecue”.

Album: Hollow Knight
“Hollow Knight” is a 2D Metroidvania action-adventure game that takes place in Hallownest, a fictional ancient kingdom. Chris Larkin composed an incredible instrumental soundtrack that helps the game come to life for the player. The serene sound is described by fans to evoke the feeling of chills.

Our Favourite Song
Musetech’s favourite song from the album is “Greenpath,” a soothing song that creates a feeling of nostalgia. After giving this song and the soundtrack a listen, it is clear that Chris is an extremely talented composer.

Olivia Rodrigo

Album: SOUR

Olivia Rodrigo is a singer-songwriter, who at just 18 years of age, has become perhaps the biggest new pop star. After releasing her first...Read More single, Drivers License, in January of 2021, this artist has broken countless records including the record for the most songs in the top 10 at one time and the most streams for a song in a single week.

Album: SOUR
Her debut album, SOUR was released in May of 2021. The album features a collection of lightly chaotic breakup tunes with extremely catchy lyrics and melodies. Each track draws you in and invites you to sing along. Simply put, it is fun to listen to. The album has a nice balance of playfulness and sullen moments.

Our Favourite Song
Musetech’s favourite song from the album is “good 4 u.” In this angsty pop-rock track, Olivia calls out an ex who quickly moves on from her, with seemingly little care. The story-line and clever lyrics make it impossible to not want to belt out each verse along with the young artist. We love high energy.
